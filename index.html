The Rating System

INTRODUCING NOUS.

NOUS stands for Net Outcome & Upset Score. Tipping the favourite barely moves it. Calling an upset correctly rockets it. The system rewards genuine knowledge — not crowd-following. Your NOUS doesn't reset each season.

NOUS Rating Tier
0 – 999 Punter
1,000 – 1,199 Follower
1,200 – 1,399 Caller
1,400 – 1,599 Reader
1,600 – 1,799 Scout
1,800 – 1,999 Oracle
2,000+ Nous
Where do you sit?

Most tippers land in Caller or Reader. The jump from Scout to Oracle is where genuine knowledge separates from lucky streaks. Reaching Nous is rare — and meaningful.

Tipping delta is always signed: +14 NOUS, −8 NOUS. Ratings are always comma-formatted: 1,672.
